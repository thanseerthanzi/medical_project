Why Choose Microascend?

We offer well-experienced and skilled faculty, modern air-conditioned laboratories equipped with advanced technology, and personalized attention to help each student grow. Our support includes thesis writing and presentation guidance, affordable accommodation assistance, Wi-Fi facilities, a seminar hall for discussions, and professional placement assistance.

About Us

MICROASCEND TESTING CENTRE AND RESEARCH EDU HUB is an ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001-2015 certified organization devoted in research and training in Microbiology, Biochemistry and other life science streams. We provide advanced research support service, internship programmes, short term practical training courses and Academic project work for industries / Research scholars and Life Science Students.
